About Me

Anthony Velardo

I lived my whole life in the small town of Palisades Park, NJ. In May of 2015, I graduated from Rutgers University with a degree in Exercise Science, and intended on becoming a Physical Therapist. My friend, who is a web developer, showed me the behind the scenes of making a website, which I found to be interesting. After fooling around on CodeAcademy my interest in web developing grew while my passion for Physical Therapy dwindled. I joined Rutgers Coding Bootcamp because I believe a classroom setting will be best for me to garner the skills to become a success in this field, not to mention my love Rutgers.

I'm very into exercising. I believe that no amount of money is as valuable, or feels as good as being healthy. Sports are another hobby of mine. If I have to pick a favorite sport it would be football, but I enjoy watching all sports in general.
